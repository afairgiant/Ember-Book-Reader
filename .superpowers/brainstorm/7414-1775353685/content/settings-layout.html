Key design decisions:
• Header card shows logo + connection status badge
• Connected Servers at top with per-server status dots
• Toggle items show switches inline, navigation items show chevrons
• Sync Settings groups frequency/highlights/bookmarks behind a single chevron row (expandable later)
• About section at bottom with dev log on long-press
• Bottom nav: Home | Browse | Library | Settings
